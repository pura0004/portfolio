ABOUT Jishnu Purahoo

Jishnu Purahoo is an Ottawa-based multimedia creator, he is currently enrolled at Algonquin College in the Interactive Media Design program. He is an international student from Mauritius. The latter has worked various jobs in the media industry over the last few years, with a particular passion for photography and graphic design. Other industry skill he possesses include web development, UX design and creative writing. In addition, Jishnu currently speak 4 languages which includes: English, French, Mauritian Creole & Hindi.

The IMD is Jishnu's second program; he has a Bachelor's degree in Journalism with a specialization in politics from the University of Mauritius. After receiving his dregree, he worked as a news reporter in Mauritius. Later joined an Events & Marketing company as a media and communication officer and later joined an advertising company as graphic designer/media co-ordinator. But later, wanted to learn more and decided to join the IMD program to learn more and be more creative.

During his free, you can find Jishnu doing what he does best: being creative, travel and explore the beautiful city of Ottawa. He also like playing video games and cooking.
